A Letter For You

💌
written on a night I couldn't stop thinking about you ✨
My dearest, my favorite person in the whole world,

I don't even know where to begin, because every time I try to put into words what you mean to me, my brain just turns into one giant "I love you" and forgets how to form actual sentences. So bear with me, okay?

You are my best friend. Like, genuinely, embarrassingly, completely my best friend. You're the first person I want to text when something funny happens, when something sad happens, when absolutely nothing happens and I just miss you. Which is always. I always miss you.

The distance is hard. I won't pretend it isn't. Some nights I look at my phone and wish so badly I could just reach through the screen and hold your hand. But then you send me a voice note, or a silly photo, or just "hey" — and somehow everything feels okay again. You have that power. You've always had it.

I want you to know that every single day I choose you. Every sleepy good morning and every "text me when you're home safe." Every time we're on call doing nothing but existing near each other. That's my favorite thing. Just existing with you.

You make me laugh louder than anyone. You make me feel softer and braver at the same time. You are the most beautiful human being I've ever seen — and yes, I get flustered every time you send a photo, every single time, don't @ me — but more than that, your heart? The way you care? The way you think? It absolutely wrecks me in the best way.

We're going to close this distance someday. And on that day, I'm going to hug you so long you'll have to physically remove me. Fair warning.

Until then, this little website is yours. All these songs are ours. And every day that passes is one day closer to you.

Yours, completely and ridiculously,
your person 💕
P.S. — You're so pretty it's actually unfair. Just saying. 😤💖

If I Were There...

🌹

close your eyes for a second. here's what i'd do if the distance just... disappeared.

🌅
first thing in the morning
I'd wake up before you just to watch you sleep for a little while. You're unfairly beautiful even then. Especially then. I'd brush the hair off your face and think — yeah, I'm the luckiest.
☕
making you coffee
I'd learn exactly how you like it — temperature, sweetness, everything — and make it for you every single morning without being asked. It'd be my favorite part of the day.
🤝
holding your hand
Everywhere. Grocery store, crossing the street, watching a movie, doing absolutely nothing. I'd hold your hand so often you'd have to tell me to stop. I wouldn't stop.
🎶
dancing with you
In the kitchen, at 2am, to no music at all. I'd spin you around and you'd laugh at me and I'd be completely, embarrassingly in love with you in that exact moment.
🌙
late nights together
We'd stay up way too late talking about everything and nothing. You'd fall asleep mid-sentence and I'd pull a blanket over you and just... stay. Because being near you is enough. More than enough.
💌
leaving you little notes
In your jacket pocket. On the mirror. Inside whatever book you're reading. Little "I love you"s and "you're my favorite person"s everywhere. So even when I'm not there, I kind of am.
